Oatly daily

I have so much love for this delicious cake! It’s in a league of it’s own as far as taste–it’s uniquely delicious, and most people haven’t tried it before and go crazy for it!
This cake can definitely be made in advance. In fact, it tastes just as good if not better made one day ahead of time.

    Ingredients:
  • 300ml cups boiling water
  • 300g oat flour
  • 50g oats
  • 20g butter
  • 20g sweetner
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • a pinch of kosher salt
  • ground cinnamon
    Options of toppings to add into the batch:
  • Banana - cut in cubes/ mashed
  • Dried fruit (e.g. raisins, banana chips, dates, cranberries)
  • Nuts (e.g. walnuts, almonds, pistachios)
  • Chocolate chips
    Here’s how to do it:
  • Step 1
    Pour the boiling water over the oats and let stand for 20 minutes.
  • Step 2
    Preheat oven. Lightly grease one 13x15 inch baking pan .
  • Step 3
    Cream the shortening with the sugar until light. Beat in the eggs. Then add the oats and vanilla, mixing well.
  • Step 4
    Combine the baking soda, salt , cinnamon and oat flour. Mix until combined. Add the toppings to the oat flour mixture and coat well.
  • Step 5
    Add oat flour mixture to the oatmeal mixture and stir to combine. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  • Step 6
    Bake at 175 degrees C for 25 minutes or until a tester inserted near the center comes out clean. Dust with confectioners' sugar or serve with whipped topping, if desired.
